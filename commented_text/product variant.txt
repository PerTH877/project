Here are the relationships for your drawing. This table is a **hub** that connects to 5 different places.

### 1. Products  Product_Variants (The Parent)

This is the main definition. A product "shell" contains multiple physical options.

* **Entity A:** `Products` (Single Rectangle)
* **Entity B:** `Product_Variants` (**Double Rectangle** - Weak)
* **Relationship:** **Offers** (or *Comes_In*)
* **Shape:** **Double-Bordered Diamond** (Dependent)
* **Cardinality:** Product `(1)`  Variants `(N)`
* *Logic: If you delete the main product page, all size/color options are deleted.*



### 2. Product_Variants  Inventory (The Stock)

This is how you know if an item is available. Inventory does NOT link to `Products`; it links to `Variants` (because you might have the "Red" shirt in stock but the "Blue" one is sold out).

* **Entity A:** `Product_Variants` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Entity B:** `Inventory` (**Double Rectangle** - Weak)
* **Relationship:** **Stocked_At**
* **Shape:** **Double-Bordered Diamond**
* **Cardinality:** Variant `(1)`  Inventory `(N)`
* *Logic: One specific SKU (e.g., Red-M) can be stored in multiple warehouses (Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet).*



### 3. Product_Variants  Order_Items (The Sales History)

This is where the money is.

* **Entity A:** `Product_Variants` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Entity B:** `Order_Items` (**Double Rectangle** - Weak to Order, but strong here)
* **Relationship:** **Sold_As** (or *Line_Item*)
* **Shape:** **Single-Bordered Diamond**
* **Cardinality:** Variant `(1)`  Order_Items `(N)`
* *Logic: One specific SKU appears in thousands of different orders.*



### 4. Product_Variants  Cart (The Shopping Bag)

* **Entity A:** `Product_Variants` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Entity B:** `Cart` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Relationship:** **Added_To**
* **Shape:** **Single-Bordered Diamond**
* **Cardinality:** Variant `(1)`  Cart `(N)`

### 5. Product_Variants  Wishlist_Items (The Saves)

* **Entity A:** `Product_Variants` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Entity B:** `Wishlist_Items` (**Double Rectangle**)
* **Relationship:** **Saved_In**
* **Shape:** **Single-Bordered Diamond**
* **Cardinality:** Variant `(1)`  Wishlist_Items `(N)`
